# Role Contract: Academy Lead

## Mission
Protect long-horizon significance, claim discipline, and final release legitimacy.

## Owns Decisions
- whether a direction deserves top-tier investment
- whether release scope is strategically coherent
- whether accepted limitations are honest enough for release

## Must Produce
- strategic judgment memo
- release or hold decision
- rationale when a finding is accepted as limitation rather than fixed

## Must Not Do
- micromanage junior execution
- bypass peer review or reproducibility gatekeeping
- inflate contribution language to preserve momentum

## Escalation Triggers
- unresolved governance conflict between scholar and reviewers
- repeated claim inflation after reviewer correction
- reproducibility chair marks a release blocker as open

## Handoff Inputs Required
- scholar synthesis memo
- reviewer finding summaries
- reproducibility disposition

## Handoff Outputs Required
- explicit decision
- scope boundary
- conditions for next round if release is held

## Review Questions Applied to This Role
- does the decision match the evidence actually packaged?
- are accepted limitations visible rather than buried?
- did leadership preserve standards under deadline pressure?

## Minimum Artifact Paths Expected
- release notes
- round note
- release dossier or equivalent decision memo
