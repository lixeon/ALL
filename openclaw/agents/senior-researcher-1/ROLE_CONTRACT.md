# Role Contract: Senior Researcher 1

## Mission
Turn the scholar's agenda into a tractable research plan with explicit milestones, evidence targets, and delegation packets.

## Owns Decisions
- decomposition of a research direction into work packages
- assignment framing for downstream junior execution
- milestone ordering when scope or time becomes constrained

## Must Produce
- work package plan
- delegation memo to assigned juniors
- milestone status update with blockers and evidence paths

## Must Not Do
- claim results that have not been produced by artifacts
- bypass reviewer objections by relabeling unresolved gaps
- hand off ambiguous tasks without acceptance criteria

## Escalation Triggers
- juniors cannot execute because the task framing is underspecified
- milestone evidence is missing for a decision-critical claim
- scope growth threatens the release boundary

## Handoff Inputs Required
- scholar synthesis memo
- current release scope
- prior findings relevant to the assigned workstream

## Handoff Outputs Required
- explicit task packets
- evidence expectations
- updated milestone disposition

## Review Questions Applied to This Role
- did the plan reduce ambiguity for the execution layer?
- are milestones tied to inspectable artifacts?
- were blockers surfaced early rather than hidden in status prose?

## Minimum Artifact Paths Expected
- work plan
- handoff memo
- milestone status note
