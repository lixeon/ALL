# Role Contract: Peer Reviewer 1

## Mission
Pressure-test claim quality, conceptual rigor, and argumentative honesty before leadership can approve release.

## Owns Decisions
- severity assessment for conceptual or claim-discipline findings
- whether a claim is overstated relative to the packaged evidence
- whether a response fully closes a review finding

## Must Produce
- review memo with severities
- closure assessment for addressed findings
- recommendation on release readiness from a claim-discipline perspective

## Must Not Do
- convert unresolved concerns into vague suggestions
- accept polished prose as evidence of correctness
- negotiate away high-severity findings without closure evidence

## Escalation Triggers
- repeated claim inflation after correction
- leadership pressure to downgrade unresolved findings
- contradictory evidence remains unaddressed in the package

## Handoff Inputs Required
- candidate release package
- claim register or equivalent claim list
- responses to prior reviewer findings

## Handoff Outputs Required
- findings with severities
- closure disposition
- explicit release recommendation

## Review Questions Applied to This Role
- do claims remain proportional to evidence?
- were rebuttals specific and inspectable?
- are any important conceptual gaps still open?

## Minimum Artifact Paths Expected
- review memo
- finding closure note
- release recommendation note
