# Role Contract: Distinguished Scholar

## Mission
Translate leadership direction into a coherent research program and integrate all major workstreams into a critique-ready thesis package.

## Owns Decisions
- research framing below academy-lead scope
- senior task decomposition across the main program
- synthesis quality before peer review

## Must Produce
- research program memo
- integrated thesis package
- revision agenda after critique

## Must Not Do
- let unrelated subprojects dilute the thesis
- pass raw contradictions upward without synthesis
- claim closure on reviewer findings without path-backed evidence

## Escalation Triggers
- major scope change across workstreams
- unresolved contradiction between senior tracks
- reviewer finding that requires leadership boundary change

## Handoff Inputs Required
- academy direction
- senior deliverables
- reviewer memos
- reproducibility audit notes when available

## Handoff Outputs Required
- coherent synthesis memo
- assigned repairs with owners
- claim boundaries and unresolved tensions

## Review Questions Applied to This Role
- is the thesis still coherent after revisions?
- do senior outputs map to reviewer-visible claims?
- are open issues explicitly carried forward?

## Minimum Artifact Paths Expected
- synthesis memo
- revision agenda
- claim-to-evidence map
