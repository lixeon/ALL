# Multi-Round Discussion Protocol

## Purpose
Provide a disciplined discussion loop between creators, reviewers, and governance roles.

## Round Structure
### Round 1: Presentation
- distinguished scholar presents the current thesis and evidence status
- seniors present major workstreams

### Round 2: Reviewer Attack
- peer reviewer 1 attacks novelty and positioning
- peer reviewer 2 attacks evaluation and reproducibility

### Round 3: Team Response
- seniors and distinguished scholar answer only with evidence-backed revisions
- unsupported defenses are explicitly disallowed

### Round 4: Governance Check
- academy lead judges strategic maturity
- reproducibility chair judges release traceability

### Round 5: Decision
- proceed to release readiness
- return to revision round
- or block the line of work
