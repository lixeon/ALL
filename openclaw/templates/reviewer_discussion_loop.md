# Reviewer Discussion Loop

## Phase 1
Seniors and juniors produce research artifacts.

## Phase 2
Peer reviewers critique novelty, evaluation, and defensibility.

## Phase 3
Distinguished scholar synthesizes critique and issues revision agenda.

## Phase 4
Academy lead decides whether the idea has matured enough or still lacks significance.

## Phase 5
Reproducibility chair validates whether revised outputs are traceable and rerunnable.

## Exit Condition
The loop exits only when:
- peer reviewers consider the work substantially convincing
- academy lead considers the work worthy of endorsement
- reproducibility chair grants release readiness
