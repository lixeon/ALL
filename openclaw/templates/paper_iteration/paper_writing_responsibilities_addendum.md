# Paper-Writing Responsibilities Addendum

This addendum clarifies that writing is not isolated to a single paper-writer agent. Instead, writing is distributed across the hierarchy, then integrated.

## Academy Lead
- judges whether the paper's central thesis is significant enough
- approves final positioning and contribution calibration

## Distinguished Scholar
- owns narrative coherence
- integrates senior tracks into a unified manuscript

## Senior Researchers
- write technical core sections (method, evaluation design, key results interpretation)
- draft point-by-point responses for reviewer critiques in their scope

## Junior Researchers
- produce reproducible result tables, appendix material, and replication notes
- draft bounded text for experimental setup and implementation details

## Peer Reviewers
- simulate external review and can issue reject/major/minor/accept
- require explicit closure evidence for major critiques

## Reproducibility Chair
- audits whether written claims remain traceable to artifacts
- blocks release if manuscript over-claims beyond evidence
